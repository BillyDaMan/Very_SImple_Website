What We Do


We take advantage of limited space and elements of the earth

by installing a wind turbine in your back yard. Although they may

be big, the turbine themselves width wise is not too space demanding.




Why Bother?


With one of the worlds most efficient renewable energy source, you

can start saving money today on your electricity bill by having a

turbine installed today. Turbines never stop, no matter if it's day

or night. Loose power due to a cyclone? No worries! The turbine

alone will be able to produce power to the necessities comfortably

during a blackout
